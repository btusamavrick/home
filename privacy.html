Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 11, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to GameVerse ("we," "us," "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your information when you use our website, services, and platform (collectively, the "Services"). Our Services are designed to provide a premier competitive gaming experience, and the data we collect is essential for operating our platform, ensuring fair play, and fostering a vibrant community. By creating an account or using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data Collection Practices

We collect information to provide and improve our Services. The types of information we collect can be broken down into several categories:

  • Account Information: When you register for a GameVerse account, we collect personal identifiers such as your chosen username, email address, and password. You may also voluntarily provide additional information, such as a profile picture or links to your social media accounts.
  • Game and Performance Data: To operate our leaderboards and tournaments, we collect data related to your in-game activity. This includes your in-game username (Gamertag), match history, win/loss records, scores, and other performance metrics (e.g., K/D ratio, accuracy). This data is collected either through direct integration with game developer APIs or through user-submitted results.
  • Technical Information: We automatically collect certain technical data when you access our Services. This includes your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device information, and usage data such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and clickstream data. This information helps us understand how users interact with our platform, diagnose technical issues, and improve our Services.
  • Communication Data: If you contact our support team, participate in our forums, or use our chat features, we collect the information you provide in your communications. This includes support tickets, forum posts, and chat logs.
  • Payment Information: For users subscribing to our premium services (Vanguard, Titan), we use third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) to handle transactions. We do not store your full credit card number or financial details on our servers. We only receive a transaction confirmation, which may include your name and the last four digits of your card.

3. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, all aimed at delivering a world-class competitive gaming experience:

  • To Provide and Maintain our Services: Your account and game data are essential for creating and managing tournaments, updating leaderboards, and providing personalized player profiles.
  • To Ensure Competitive Integrity: We analyze gameplay data and use technical information to detect and prevent cheating, fraud, and other violations of our Terms of Service. This is critical to maintaining a fair playing field for all users.
  • To Communicate with You: We use your email address to send important service-related announcements, such as tournament reminders, password resets, and updates to our policies. With your consent, we may also send you marketing communications about new features, games, and events.
  • To Improve and Personalize the Services: We analyze usage data to understand user behavior and preferences. This allows us to improve the user interface, develop new features, and personalize the content and recommendations you see.
  • To Provide Customer Support: We use communication data to respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot problems, and provide assistance.

4. User Rights and Controls

You have rights and control over your personal data. At GameVerse, we empower you to manage your information:

  • Access and Correction: You can access and update most of your account information at any time through your 'Account Settings' page.
  • Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated personal data. Please note that for reasons of competitive integrity, some anonymized gameplay data (e.g., match results without your username) may be retained. Furthermore, we may be required to retain certain information for legal or security purposes.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You can unsubscribe from our marketing emails at any time by clicking the 'unsubscribe' link at the bottom of each email.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our support team via email at privacy@gameverse.com.

5. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve our Services. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them for:

  • Essential Functions: To keep you logged in and maintain your session.
  • Preferences: To remember your settings, such as your preferred language or theme.
  • Analytics: To help us understand how you use our Services, so we can improve them. We use services like Google Analytics for this purpose.

You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. However, if you choose to disable cookies, it may limit your use of certain features or functions on our Services.

6. Third-Party Services

We may share your data with third-party vendors, service providers, and partners who perform services on our behalf. This includes payment processors, cloud hosting providers (like AWS or Google Cloud), and analytics services. We have strict data processing agreements in place with these third parties to ensure they only use your information for the purposes we've authorized and that they provide a level of protection for your data that is consistent with this policy.
